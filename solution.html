Solution to improve maternal health and discovering the technologies behind!


Receiving a bracelet

  • As a woman is diagnosed to be pregnant, she can lend a bracelet and a blood test box from the hospital in the developing countries.
  • For those who are able to purchase the bracelet in the developed countries, they are encouraged to equip themselves with the bracelet to optimize their health condition during pregnancy.
  • The data obtained from the full body scanner and the medical history of the woman will be imported into the bracelet and synchronized with the medical database in the hospital.

  • Technology behind:Scanner


Blood Test

  • During the second trimester of pregnancy, blood test is essential to ensure good health.
  • Due to inconvenient transportation in rural area,the “Blood Test in a Box” technology is apllied for blood test.
  • The mothers can collect the few drops of blood sample needed by themselves.
  • The bracelet will schedule the collection of the blood sample for laboratory analysis by the drone.
  • When the results are available, data will be sent through Internet in the developed countries while the developing countries uses SMS.
  • The AI in the bracelet will explain the result with the aid of hologram projection.

  • Technology behind:“Blood Test in a Box”
    Artificial Intelligence(AI)


Long-term Caring

  • The bracelet can propagate the theta waves which can soothe the emotion.
  • For regular health check-up, the AI will schedule and remind the mothers to perform a whole body scan and ultrasound scan.

Delivery and Confinement Phase

  • An emergency button which works with the AI is provided to ensure the mothers can get help in the shortest time if any unexpected situation happens.
  • The mobile maternal waiting service center will be built and patrol around in the rural area and there are AI nursing robot with human surveillance.
  • The robot which presintalled with the knowledge of Gynecology will be sent to the mother’s home to prepare for labour.
  • The maternal waiting service center will offer the AI robot connected to “Raphael” using Bluetooth to take care of the mothers and babies during confinement phase.
  • The diet planning and constant health check are the musts after mother giving birth to the baby.
